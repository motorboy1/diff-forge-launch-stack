# Privacy Policy

**Effective Date:** February 20, 2026
**Last Updated:** February 20, 2026

---

## 1. Overview

DiffForge ("we", "us") respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy describes how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our services.

## 2. Information We Collect

### Information You Provide
- **Account information:** Email address, name, organization name
- **Payment information:** Processed by third-party payment provider; we do not store credit card numbers
- **Communication:** Messages sent through support channels

### Information Collected Automatically
- **Usage data:** Pages visited, assets downloaded, features used
- **Device information:** Browser type, operating system, screen resolution
- **Log data:** IP address, access timestamps, referring URLs

## 3. How We Use Your Information

- Process purchases and deliver assets
- Send transaction confirmations and subscription notifications
- Improve our services based on aggregate usage patterns
- Provide customer support
- Detect and prevent fraud

We do **not**:
- Sell your personal information to third parties
- Share your data with advertisers
- Track your behavior across other websites
- Use your data for AI model training

## 4. Data Retention

- Account data: Retained while your account is active
- Transaction records: Retained for 5 years (legal compliance)
- Usage analytics: Aggregated and anonymized after 12 months
- Support communications: Retained for 2 years

## 5. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures including:
- TLS encryption for all data in transit
- Encrypted storage for data at rest
- Access controls limited to authorized personnel
- Regular security audits

## 6. Your Rights

You have the right to:
- **Access** your personal data
- **Correct** inaccurate information
- **Delete** your account and associated data
- **Export** your data in a portable format
- **Restrict** processing of your data

To exercise these rights, contact: privacy@diffforge.dev

## 7. Cookies

We use essential cookies for:
- Session management
- Authentication state
- Purchase flow completion

We do not use tracking cookies or third-party analytics cookies.

## 8. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:
- **Payment processing:** Secure payment provider (PCI DSS compliant)
- **Hosting:** Cloud infrastructure provider
- **Email:** Transactional email service for notifications

Each third-party service has its own privacy policy.

## 9. International Transfers

Your data may be processed in countries outside the Republic of Korea. We ensure adequate protection through standard contractual clauses.

## 10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated effective date.
